Our Story

A Sanctuary of Taste in the Heart of Fort Portal

Nestled in the lush highlands of Fort Portal, Cafe De Forte was born from a single conviction: that extraordinary food and remarkable ambiance should not be a privilege reserved for distant cities.

We are part café, part bakery, part fine-dining lounge — and entirely yours. From the first pour of our artisan morning brew to the last bite of an evening meal, every detail is crafted to make you feel that this place was made for you.

Our kitchen draws from bold Indian-inspired curries, hearty African comfort meals, and wood-fired pizzas — united by one philosophy: quality without compromise.
